Thoughtful details

What is the average price per person?

A full dinner typically ranges from €90 to €140 per person. Our tasting menu is €110, with an optional wine pairing at €55.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy requirements. Share any needs when booking.

Can you host private groups or events?

Yes. We host private terraces for 8–20 guests and full buyouts for special occasions.

Is there parking available?

Complimentary valet parking is available every evening. Boat arrivals can dock at our private pier.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request 24 hours’ notice for changes or cancellations. Peak-season late cancellations may incur a €30 fee per guest.
